### Legislation or Bill(s)

State Archives and Records Commission established a retention schedule for camera recordings

### Collection of Video

None

### Retention of Video

Non-evidentiary recordings are retained for 30 days. Recordings of DUI-related incidents must be retained for 14 months, but if an accident is recorded, they are retained for 26 months

### FOIA Exemptions

Covered by General FOIA Exemptions

### Related Dashcam FOIA Exemption

Covered by General FOIA Exemptions
